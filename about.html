Paul Turec

President & CEO

Paul launched Rocketship Strategies to help early and mid-stage companies gain access to high-performing Sales, Channel and Marketing leadership to help them scale and grow without having to make the commitment of the time, money and effort required to invest in industry veterans at this stage in their careers to join their business.

With deep experience in SaaS, Fintech, Payments and Logistics, leading multiple Sales, Channel and revenue growth strategies domestically and internationally for over two-decades, Paul was able to take advantage of riding the growth rocketship at multiple companies who went IPO or were later acquired by larger, public companies due to their explosive growth.

He brings critical experience and a wealth of knowledge necessary to help founders and leadership teams build their own rocketships capable of achieving any heights their businesses and dreams can take them.
